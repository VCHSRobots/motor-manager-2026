About This System

Welcome to the EPIC Robotz Motor Management System. This platform helps our team track, test, and manage all motors used in our robotics competitions.

Key features include:

  • Complete motor inventory tracking
  • Performance test data storage and analysis
  • Motor status and lifecycle management
  • Historical performance tracking
